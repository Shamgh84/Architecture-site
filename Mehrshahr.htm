Mehrshahr

Type: Residential Penthouse Interior Design

Location: Karaj, Iran

Status: Under Construction

Unit Design: Shabnam Ghadyani

Lobby Design: Sahar Najafi

Year: 2019

This residential project is part of a 14–15-story building characterized by wooden terraces that extend inward, creating a soft, organic connection between the exterior and interior spaces. Inspired by these flowing forms, I incorporated gentle lines and smooth shapes throughout the design to achieve visual harmony.

The unit, located on the 11th and 12th floors, features an oval-shaped void and a spiral staircase that elegantly connects the two levels, emphasizing the spaciousness of the penthouse.

To maintain coherence with the warm wooden terraces, the interior palette consists of warm tones and natural materials such as wood, creating a sense of comfort and continuity. The lighting design follows the same philosophy, featuring curved and distinctive forms that enhance the fluid aesthetic of the space.

Every detail, from the bespoke kitchen counters and cabinets to the carefully selected furniture, was designed to balance luxury with warmth — offering an inviting yet refined atmosphere.
