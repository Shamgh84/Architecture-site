Delbar Khaneh

Type: Boutique Hotel

Location: Yazd , Iran

Status: Under Construction

Design Team: Sahar Najafi, Shabnam Ghadyani

Principal Architects: Nima Keivani, Sina Keivani

Year: 2020

Awards: A' Design Award and Competition

The Delbar Khaneh Boutique Hotel project is nestled in the historical fabric of the city of Yazd; a dazzling jewel remaining from the Afshari era. It's a building that was never completed, and over time, it has inscribed its stories on its walls - from the scars of its designs to damages caused by natural and human factors.

The Keivani Architects team, with a shining record in the restoration and rehabilitation of historical sites, took on this project with the aim of reviving the soul of this structure and enhancing its beauty. They intend to upgrade the interior design in a modern and charming way while preserving its historical aspects.

The concept of love and affection is clearly evident in the architecture of the building. Every corner of this house whispers the unfinished stories of love, like a lover who never reached union with their beloved.

The Delbar Khaneh Boutique Hotel consists of seven separate rooms, each with its own unique design and concept, crafted to invite guests on a journey through time and space. The details and furnishings of these rooms have been meticulously and artistically designed by the Kiavani Architects' Industrial Design Department, in harmony with the specific concepts of each room.

This project is not just an architectural renovation but an embodiment of history, culture, and love that breathes and gives life in every corner of Delbar Khaneh.
